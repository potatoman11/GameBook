Game Reviews

Fortnite is a game defending against zombie like figures and also their very popular mode, Battle Royale. Popular players include Twitch.BlooTea, Ninja and Sevennoss.Fortnite is run by epic games. 2. The Witcher 3:Wild Hunt / Ratings:4.8/5

Witcher 3 is an online action Role Playing video game.Developed and published by CD Projekt, The Witcher 3 was based off of a series of novels by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. The Witcher 3 is played on a PS4 and is a sequel to The Witcher 2:Assasins of Kings. The Witcher can also be played on a Xbox and Microsoft Windows. In Third Person Perspective, players control Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher.

3. Minecraft / Ratings:4/5

Minecraft is an old game originally developed by Notch (aka Markus Persson) and later developed by Mojang. It is a sandbox game and began as a hobby. It allows players to build with an assortment of blocks. Other topics in the game include exploration, crafting and combat. By 2018, 144 million copies have been sold. Minecon conventions also add to the game's popularity. Minecraft can be played on Xbox, tablet, ipad, phone and a Mac.

4. Player Unknown's Battlegrounds (Pub G) / Ratings:4.3/5

Pub G is a Battle Royale game developed by PUB G Corporations. It is a third person shooter game and is similiar to Fortnite Battle Royale, except more realistic and not that cartoony. Pub G is based off of a last man standing concept, with 100 players going into a battle and one player winning. Pub G was released in 2017 for Steam on Windows, and later released for Xbox/ other devices. Popular players are Anthony_KongPhan, JackFrags and Lirik.

5. No Man's Sky / Ratings: 4/5

No Man's Sky is and action adventure survival game developed by the indie studio Hello Games. It was released worldwide on August 2016 for PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows. In No Man's Sky, players can perform in free space with over 18 quintillion planets waitinh to be explored. Planes even have their own ecosytems with their own unique forms of fauna and flora.

6. The Legends Of Zelda: Breath Of The Wild / Ratings: 5/5

Breath of the Wild is an action adventure styled game taking place in an open world enviroment. Developed by nintendo, it was released in 2017 for Nintendo Switch and WiiU. Players are tasked for exploring the kingdom of Hyrule while controlling a character named Link. Hyrule has an ongoing conflict with the evil Calamity Ganon. Breath Of The Wild was released after Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword.

7. OverWatch / Ratings: 4.7/5

Overwatch is a team-battle multiplayer first person shooter developed by Blizzard Entertainment which was released on 2016 for PlayStation 4, Xbox and Windows. Overwatch places players in two teams of six, each selecting between a roster of 20 players. OverWatch is one of Blizzard's major successes, following their cancellation of the 2014 multiplayer online role playing game Titan. Popular OverWatch players (by player ID) are Gesture, Profit, Closer and birdring.

8. World Of Warcraft (WoW) / Ratings: 4/5

World of Warcraft is a massive multiplayer online role playing game developed by blizzard entertainment. It is the fourth game in the Warcraft series. Like other MMORPGs, players control an avater in third or first person view. To join, players must select one out of four server options: Normal(player versus enviroment or PvE)where players focus more on monsters and quests, PvP (player versus player) where in addition to PvE open combat is allowed, RP (roleplay) where players roleplay in character, and RP-PvP (roleplay PvP) where players are in character and cross faction combat is allowed.

9. Horizon Zero Dawn / Ratings: 5/5

Horizon Zero Dawn is a role playing action game developed by Geurilla Games and released in early 2017 for Play Station 4. The plot is all about Aloy who is a hunter living in a world overrun by machines. Aloy uses ranged weapons, stealth and a spear to combat the robots. Some machines move in herds or flocks which make it more difficult to go in all out combat. There are three choices in the skill tree: Prowler affects stealth, Brave improves combat and Forager increases healing and gathering. Zero Dawn comes with and open world enviroment with a day and night cycle and seperate landscape regions with their own weather system.

10. Monster Hunter: World / Ratings: 5/5

Once again, Monster Hunter is an action role playing game and is developed byCapcom. It was released world wide in 2018 for Play Station 4 and Xbox. A Windows version is scheduled for release in August. The player controls a hunter thay can name supported by an assistant handler. Like plots from previous games, the hunter travels to the "New World" which is an unpopulated land mass filled with monsters. The Research Commision orders the hunter to hunt large monsters to protect the commision and for research while equipped with either a longbow, sword or hammer. Weapon types define the abilities and skills that players can later use.

11. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim / 4.7/5

Like the last three games listed, The Elder Scrolls V is an action role playing game, in either a first person or third person perspective. Players roam freely over the land of Skyrim, an open world enviroment consisting of wilderness, dungeons, cities, towns, fortresses and villages. NPCs (non player characters) populate the world, interacting with the characters. They can also offer the player side quests and cannot be killed due to their importance in later storylines. At the beginning of the game, players choose between several races: orcs, elves, humans and anthropomorphic cat or lizard like creatures. Developed by Bethesda Game Studios, Elder Scrolls is made for Windows, PS4, PS3, Xbox 360, Xbox 1 and Nintendo Switch.

14. TitanFall 2

TitanFall 2 is a first person shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and like TitanFall, TitanFall 2 allows players to control the titan and their pilots. TitanFall 2 was released in 2016 for Microsoft Windows, Xbox and PlayStation 4. Pilots have abilities such as cloaking, grappling and parkour which allow them to increase their Titan's effiency during combat. TitanFall 2 also introduces several new movements. Pilots also have an arsenal of weapons such as shotguns, submachine guns, pistols and grenades to take their enemies without a Titan. Titans, mecha style exoskeletons/giant robots, have less mobility than pilots but with massively increased armor and weapon damage. TitanFall 2 has a single player and multiplayer. Single player consists of story campaign. Multiplayer introduces a titan meter, allowing the player to unlock a titan when the meter is full. Multiplayer modes include Amped Hardpoint (Teams receive point for holding control spots, most point wins), Bounty Hunt (Players get money for killing oppenents or AI grunts; players can steal other player's money by killing them. Highest scoring team wins.), pilot VS pilot (deathmatch with no titan), capture the flag (standard capture the flag rules), Attrition (team deathmatch including AI controlled grunts), Skirmish (attrition with no grunts or score), and Last Titan Standing (standard team deathmatch mode, but players cannot eject from their Titans.)
