1. Fortnite / Ratings: 4/5

Fortnite is a cartoony third person shooter game which is especially good for people who do not like blood. Fortnite is on of the most streamed games ever and quickly rose to the top 10s. Fortnite is a game defending against zombie like figures and also their very popular mode, Battle Royale. Basically there is Fortnite: Battle Royale and Fortnite: Save the World. Save the World is about four players that all work together to suceed in the same mission. Four players take the role of commander defending homes or buildings laded with traps. Players in Save The World Save other people, defennd items and collect information about the storm. Battle Royale is a battle mode of 100 players going into a last man standing with one emerging victorious. Or another option is teams(50 versus 50, first team to get completely destroyed loses and other team wins #1 Victory Royale). Popular players include Twitch.BlooTea, Ninja and Sevennoss.Fortnite is run by epic games. You can play Fortnite on a Mac, PC, Nintendo, PS4 and Xbox.

3. Minecraft / Ratings:4/5

Minecraft is an old game originally developed by Notch (aka Markus Persson) and later developed by Mojang. It is a sandbox game and began as a hobby. It allows players to build with an assortment of blocks. Other topics in the game include exploration, crafting and combat. By 2018, 144 million copies have been sold. Hostile Minecraft mobs include Skeletons, Creepers, Zombies, Spiders, Cave Spiders, Enderman, Drowned, Endermites, Slimes, Silverfish, Witches, Shulkers, Guardians, Elder Guardians, Blazes, Zombie Pigmen, Ghasts, Magma Cubes, The Wither(boss), and The Ender Dragon.(boss) There are also many passive mobs like animals and sea creatures.

4. Player Unknown's Battlegrounds (Pub G) / Ratings:4.3/5

Pub G is a Battle Royale game developed by PUB G Corporations. It is a third person shooter game and is similiar to Fortnite Battle Royale, except more realistic and not that cartoony. Pub G is based off of a last man standing concept, with 100 players going into a battle and one player winning. Every game starts with players parachuting down onto a 8x8 kilometer map. Starting with no gear, players have to find items and weapon distributed throughout the map. Every few minutes, the playable area of the map shrinks, toward a random location, with any player outside of the safe zone qradually taking damage until death. Pub G was released in 2017 for Steam on Windows, and later released for Xbox/ other devices. Popular players are Anthony_KongPhan, JackFrags and Lirik.

5. No Man's Sky / Ratings: 4/5

No Man's Sky is and action adventure survival game developed by the indie studio Hello Games. It was released worldwide on August 2016 for PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Windows. In No Man's Sky, players can perform in free space with over 18 quintillion planets waitinh to be explored. Planes even have their own ecosytems with their own unique forms of fauna and flora. The defining features of No Man's Sky is that nearly all the parts of the qalaxy, including stars, planets, plants, alien life and encounters, are created through procedural generation using random number generators from one seed number. Due to a 64 bit value, this leads there to be 18 quintillion (18000000000000000000 or 1.8 X 1019) planets that are generated.

6. The Legends Of Zelda: Breath Of The Wild / Ratings: 5/5

Breath of the Wild is an action adventure styled game taking place in an open world enviroment. Developed by nintendo, it was released in 2017 for Nintendo Switch and WiiU. 8. World Of Warcraft (WoW) / Ratings: 4/5

World of Warcraft is a massive multiplayer online role playing game developed by blizzard entertainment. It is the fourth game in the Warcraft series. Like other MMORPGs, players control an avater in third or first person view. To join, players must select one out of four server options: Normal(player versus enviroment or PvE)where players focus more on monsters and quests, PvP (player versus player) where in addition to PvE open combat is allowed, RP (roleplay) where players roleplay in character, and RP-PvP (roleplay PvP) where players are in character and cross faction combat is allowed. Players may also join or form guilds, a team of players that help and donate to each other.

11. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

12. God of War / Ratings: 5/5

God of War is an Action Adventure video game developed by Monica Studios, built for PlayStation 4. The gameplay is vastly different from previous installments. The previous main game, Ascension, introduced multiplayer to the series. One of the major differences is that Kratos doesn't use his double chained blades anymore (The Blades of Chaos), opting instead for a magical battle axe called the Leviathan Axe. 13. Destiny 2 / Ratings: 4/5

Destiny 2 is an online only multiplayer first person shooter video game developed by bungie and very similiar to Destiny. Released in 2017 for Xbox and PlayStation 4, Destiny 2 is divided into PvE (player versus enviroment) and PvP (player versus player), like the original. Players are responsible with the role of Guardian, protectors of Earth's last safe city. A lot of things in Destiny 2 are similiar to it's predecessor. One thing different, though, is that Destiny 2 features a more optimal way of matchmaking calles 'Guided Games', which allows players to search for clan that may need additional players for activities, like strikes or raids. In the beginning of the game the player chooses a class (same as Destiny), either Warlock, Hunter or Titan. Then they select a species: Human, Awoken (blue-gray skinned descendants of humans) and Exo (humanoid robots). Each class has upgrades, perks and abilities, and each class has a subclass (Hunter has Gunslinger, Nightstalker sand Arcstrider; Warlocks have Voidwalker, Stormcaller and Dawnblade; and Titans have Striker, Sentinel and Sunbreaker).

14. TitanFall 2 / Ratings: 4/5

TitanFall 2 is a first person shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and like TitanFall, TitanFall 2 allows players to control the titan and their pilots. TitanFall 2 was released in 2016 for Microsoft Windows, Xbox and PlayStation 4. Pilots have abilities such as cloaking, grappling and parkour which allow them to increase their Titan's effiency during combat. TitanFall 2 also introduces several new movements. Pilots also have an arsenal of weapons such as shotguns, submachine guns, pistols and grenades to take their enemies without a Titan. Titans, mecha style exoskeletons/giant robots, have less mobility than pilots but with massively increased armor and weapon damage. TitanFall 2 has a single player and multiplayer. Single player consists of story campaign. Multiplayer introduces a titan meter, allowing the player to unlock a titan when the meter is full. Multiplayer modes include Amped Hardpoint (Teams receive point for holding control spots, most point wins), Bounty Hunt (Players get money for killing oppenents or AI grunts; players can steal other player's money by killing them. Highest scoring team wins.), pilot VS pilot (deathmatch with no titan), capture the flag (standard capture the flag rules), Attrition (team deathmatch including AI controlled grunts), Skirmish (attrition with no grunts or score), and Last Titan Standing (standard team deathmatch mode, but players cannot eject from their Titans.)
